Proposition: White officers are the subject of the majority of allegations, indicating a higher frequency of reports against them compared to minority officers.

FOR the Proposition

When building the chart, I utilized data from New York City OpenData (available at data.cityofnewyork.us) to get information about police precinct borders. I downloaded the geographic data as a geojson file that can be used in Tableau. This file allowed me to depict each NYPD precinct on a map, giving a clear picture of where each one is. To display the number of complaints against officers, I colored each precinct based on the ethnicity reported most often within that area. This gave me a better visual understanding of which ethnicities are most often connected to complaints in different parts of the city.
